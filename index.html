This year, I landed my first internship in full-stack engineering, at an Agri-tech startup called Farmz2u, based in Nigeria. Here, I honed my skills in Django and GitLab while collaborating remotely with my team. My top three technical skills are Python, Git, and Bash. I am also a freelance developer on Fiverr. My ultimate goal is to solve real-world problems through innovative projects and become a well-rounded engineer. I am passionate about mastering my craft and learning new tools, especially in cloud computing, cybersecurity, AI, and machine learning.

When I'm not writing code, I enjoy playing and watching basketball, as well as reading books like Atomic Habits and any Lee Child action thrillers. I am a Christian and my faith plays a big role in my life.
